Tumor Evolution Visualization
  • Upload Data

Required

  • Subject ID - A unique ID associated with the patient or source the data was derived from.
  • Day - The number of days since the first sample was acquired from the source. The day the first sample was acquired should be 0.
  • Gene - The name of the gene.
  • AA_change - The amino acid change in the form of original amino acid letter, position in peptide chain, and new amino acid letter (or deletion/insertion). An example of this is "R140Q".
  • VAF - The variant allele frequency.

Optional

  • Chr - The chromosome the gene is on.
  • Position - The position of the variant on the chromosome.
  • Total Read - The total number of reads sequenced for the particular locus.
  • Ref_read - The number of total reads that map to the reference sequence for the particular locus.
  • Var_read - The number of total reads that don't map to the reference sequence for the particular locus.
  • Alt - The alternative sequence observed at the particular locus.
  • Ref - The reference sequence for the particular locus.
  • cDNA_change - The cDNA change observed as a result of the variant.
  • VariantType - The type of mutation of the variant (i.e. Frameshift, Missense, Nonsense)
  • Assay - The type of assay used to determine the variants.
  • RefSeq - An associated RefSeq accession number.
