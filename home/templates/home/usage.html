TEV Usage Video

The TEV usage video shows all of the features available in the TEV tool. These features include selecting a patient, creating a fishplot, editing sample purity at a timepoint, generating cancer cell fractions (CCFs), saving fishplots, loading fishplots, editing variant allele frequencies, editing variant ploidy, using the variant summary statistics, and pulling up additional information on a variant.

One feature not shown in this video is selectively removing fishplots. To use this feature, right click on the fishplot you wish to remove and select "Remove fishplot(s)". By choosing this option you will remove the selected fishplot and all of its children. Additionally, one feature that may not be apparent from the video is that when you change the ploidy of a variant, all of its children or soon to be children (from plotting) will inherit the parents ploidy.
